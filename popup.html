ChatGPT for the Search Bar

Lets you quickly use ChatGPT from the search bar.

  1. Navigate to chrome://settings/searchEngines
  2. Under "Site search" clidk on "Add"
  3. Fill in the following fields:
    • Search engine: ChatGPT
    • Keyword: c (or your preferred hotkey)
    • URL with %s in place of query: https://chat.openai.com/chat?q=%s
